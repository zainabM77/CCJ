Who We Are

Canadian Community Justice (CCJ) was registered under the Not-for-Profit Corporations Act (NFP Act) to serve individuals and families across Ontario and beyond.

We are a community-based organisation dedicated to supporting access to justice, human rights education, and community well-being in Canada.

Our Mission

CCJ is a non-profit organisation dedicated to promoting access to justice, human rights awareness, community education, immigration settlement support, and the peaceful resolution of family and community conflicts.

We work with individuals and families from diverse backgrounds, empowering them through information, education, and referral-based support.

Our goal is to support refugees, displaced individuals, and those affected by conflict—both nationally and internationally.

What We Do

  • Legal & rights education
  • Immigration settlement information & referrals
  • Human rights advocacy & awareness
  • Family conflict support & mediation
  • Community capacity building
  • Advance awareness of legal rights and responsibility to the public
  • Advance of religious teaching, practice, and worship for the community
  • Advance of immigration settlement, advocacy & referrals
  • Advance of family conflict support & mediation services
  • Advance of community capacity building support, and relief of poverty
